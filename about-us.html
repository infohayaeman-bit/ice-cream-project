About Scoops with Smiles

Serving Happiness One Scoop at a Time

At Scoops with Smiles, we believe that every scoop of ice cream should bring a smile. Founded with love in Groveport, Ohio, our mission is simple: create delicious, fun, and unforgettable treats for everyone. From classic flavors to creative specials, every dessert is crafted with the freshest ingredients and served with a warm, friendly smile. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick treat or celebrating a special moment, Scoops with Smiles is your happy place for sweet memories.
